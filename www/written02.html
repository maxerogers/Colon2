Where to go on the day of the colonoscopy

The examination is done in the Endoscopy Suite, at Stony Brook University Hospital. Take the main elevators to the 14th floor. Proceed left to the North Tower to Endoscopy Reception (14N 01). The actual procedure takes approximately 1 hour, but expect to be at the hospital for about 3-5 hours.
